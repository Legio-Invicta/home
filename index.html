Dear new members of Legio Invicta, this guide presents all major events in Goodgame Empire. Whether you're a beginner or experienced player, you'll find all the information you need to excel in each event.

Beyond the Horizon

Level 30+

Requirements

  • Must be in alliance before event starts
  • Enter Horizon within first 48 hours (2-day joining window)
  • First player to register becomes leader of first Horizon Alliance (max 22 players)
  • Up to 3 Horizon alliances per main alliance (approx. 60 players total)

Objective

Occupy states and defeat opponents' capitals/castles to gain Influence. Daily influence transfers after midnight to your alliance capital, which must be defended during the final days.

Influence counts for individual and alliance leaderboards. Final alliance rank equals influence in capital at event end.

Key Features

Cult Shrine

Works like robber barons. Levels up after defeat and yields more resources next time.

Alliance Capital

Common castle for all alliance members. Can be decorated and defended together.

Influence System

Gain influence through battles and occupation. Transfers daily to capital at midnight.

State Occupation

Control regions to earn passive influence. Strategic positioning is crucial.

Winning Strategies

Early Game (Days 1-3)

  • Secure key states early for maximum passive influence
  • Focus on Cult Shrines to level them up quickly
  • Coordinate with alliance for state control
  • Build defensive structures around capital

Mid Game (Days 4-6)

  • Attack enemy capitals to steal influence
  • Maintain state occupation for daily transfers
  • Upgrade capital defenses
  • Scout enemy positions and weaknesses

End Game (Days 7-9)

  • Maximum defense around alliance capital
  • All members station defensive armies at capital
  • Focus on protecting accumulated influence
  • Counter-attack threats immediately

Pro Tips

Communication is Key: Use alliance chat constantly to coordinate attacks and defenses.

Timing Matters: Influence transfers at midnight. Plan major moves accordingly.

Individual Rankings: Personal influence counts separately. Balance helping alliance with personal goals.

Capital Defense: In final days, having everyone's armies at capital is critical.

Outer Realms

Level 70+

Requirements

  • Castle level 70 or higher
  • Must register during registration period (usually 2-3 days)
  • Alliance minimum: 10 members registered
  • Preparation time before event starts

Objective

Compete against other alliances in a separate realm to control territories, complete objectives, and accumulate points. The alliance with the most points at the end wins prestigious rewards.

This is one of the most competitive events, requiring excellent coordination and strategic planning.

Key Features

Territory Control

Capture and hold strategic points on the map for passive point generation.

Alliance Castles

Build and defend multiple castles. Each castle provides strategic advantages.

Realm Quests

Complete special objectives for bonus points and resources.

PVP Combat

Intense battles against other top alliances. Every attack matters.

Winning Strategies

Pre-Event Preparation

  • Build massive troop reserves before event starts
  • Stockpile food and resources
  • Organize alliance roles (attackers, defenders, builders)
  • Study the map and identify key territories

Opening Moves

  • Rush to capture high-value territories immediately
  • Establish strong defensive positions early
  • Complete easy quests for early point lead
  • Scout enemy alliance positions

Mid-Event Tactics

  • Focus attacks on enemy weak points
  • Coordinate timed strikes with alliance
  • Maintain supply lines to front-line castles
  • Adapt strategy based on point standings

Final Push

  • All-out offense if behind on points
  • Strong defense if leading to protect advantage
  • Complete remaining high-value quests
  • Deny enemy point-generating territories

Nomad Invasion

Level 30+

Requirements

  • Castle level 30 or higher
  • No alliance requirement (solo event)
  • Lasts approximately 7-10 days
  • Available to all eligible players

Objective

Defend your castle and surrounding territories from waves of Nomad invaders. Defeat Nomad camps and leaders to earn points and valuable rewards.

This is primarily a PvE (Player vs Environment) event, though some PvP elements exist.

Key Features

Nomad Camps

Various difficulty levels. Higher levels give better rewards but are tougher.

Khan's Fortress

Special boss camp. Requires strong army but offers best rewards.

Point System

Earn points by defeating camps. Compete on leaderboard for top rewards.

Special Units

Event-specific troops can be recruited for advantages against Nomads.

Efficient Farming Guide

Target Selection

  • Start with camps matching your army strength
  • Focus on camps with best reward-to-effort ratio
  • Save Khan's Fortress attempts for when properly prepared
  • Clear easier camps first to accumulate points

Army Composition

  • Use event units if available (often have bonuses)
  • Mix ranged and melee for balanced attacks
  • Bring siege equipment for fortified camps
  • Keep backup armies ready for multiple attacks

Point Optimization

  • Attack during point multiplier periods if available
  • Complete daily quests for bonus points
  • Form temporary alliances to take down tough camps
  • Don't waste troops on low-value targets

Pro Tips

Scout First: Always scout camps before attacking to avoid costly losses.

Refresh Camps: Camps respawn. Mark good ones and revisit them.

Resource Priority: Focus on camps dropping resources you need most.

Time Management: Plan attacks around your available play time.

Samurai Invasion

Level 10+

Requirements

  • Castle level 10 or higher
  • Must be in an alliance
  • Alliance must register for event
  • Suitable for beginners and veterans

Objective

Work together with your alliance to defeat waves of Samurai attackers. Build defensive structures, coordinate strategies, and earn alliance points to unlock rewards.

This is a cooperative alliance event focused on defense and teamwork.

Key Features

Wave Defense

Multiple attack waves increase in difficulty. Survive longer for better rewards.

Alliance Coordination

Success depends on all members contributing defenses and troops.

Defensive Structures

Build towers, walls, and traps. Strategic placement is crucial.

Milestone Rewards

Berimond

Level 40+

Requirements

  • Castle level 40 or higher
  • Choose faction: Order or Chaos
  • Faction choice is permanent for event duration
  • Typically runs 7-10 days

Objective

Join either the Order or Chaos faction and fight for supremacy in Berimond. Complete quests, defeat enemies, and help your faction control the realm.

This is a large-scale faction war event with both PvE and PvP elements.

Key Features

Faction Choice

Order (defensive) vs Chaos (offensive). Each has unique units and bonuses.

Territory War

Factions battle for control of strategic locations across Berimond.

Faction Quests

Complete objectives to earn faction points and personal rewards.

Boss Battles

Defeat powerful faction bosses with coordinated attacks.

Faction Strategies

Order Faction Strategy

  • Leverage defensive bonuses to hold territories
  • Build strong fortifications at key points
  • Excel in long-term territory control
  • Work well for defensive-minded players

Chaos Faction Strategy

  • Use offensive bonuses for aggressive expansion
  • Strike fast and capture territories quickly
  • Disrupt enemy supply lines and defenses
  • Ideal for aggressive, active players

Universal Tactics

  • Complete daily quests for consistent progress
  • Coordinate with faction members in chat
  • Focus on high-value territories
  • Balance PvE farming with PvP objectives

Winning Approach

  • Contribute to faction goals, not just personal ones
  • Participate in organized faction raids
  • Defend faction-held territories when attacked
  • Maximize point generation from all activities

Pro Tips

Faction Units: Train faction-specific units for combat advantages.

Map Awareness: Know which territories give best faction bonuses.

Team Play: Join organized faction groups for coordinated attacks.

Quest Focus: Prioritize quests with best reward-to-effort ratio.

Alliance Rules & Policies

These rules ensure fair play and harmony within Legio Invicta. All members must follow these guidelines.

General Conduct

  • Respect all alliance members and other players
  • Use appropriate language in alliance chat
  • No cheating, exploiting, or account sharing
  • Be active and contribute to alliance events
  • Help newer members learn the game

PVP Rules

  • No attacks on alliance members - Zero tolerance
  • NAP (Non-Aggression Pact) Alliances: Check alliance threads for current NAPs
  • Retaliation Policy: Report attacks to leaders before retaliating
  • Territory Respect: Don't place outposts too close to allied castles
  • War Protocol: Follow leadership directions during wars

Event Participation

  • Mandatory Events: Beyond the Horizon and Outer Realms
  • Communication: Update leaders if you can't participate
  • Coordination: Follow alliance event strategies
  • Contribution: Meet minimum event point requirements
  • Resources: Share resources with alliance when needed

Activity Requirements

  • Login Frequency: At least once every 3 days
  • Event Participation: Active in major alliance events
  • Extended Absence: Notify leaders if away more than 5 days
  • Inactivity Kick: 7 days without notice = removal

Resource & Trading

  • Fair trading rates with alliance members
  • Priority resource sharing during events
  • No resource draining (joining just to take resources)
  • Contribute to alliance construction projects

Communication

  • Alliance Chat: Check regularly for important updates
  • Discord/External: Join alliance Discord if available
  • Announcements: Read all official announcements
  • Feedback: Constructive feedback to leaders is welcomed

Safehouse Policy

What is a Safehouse?
A protected zone where alliance members can relocate during wars or when under heavy attack.

Rules:

  • Use only when genuinely threatened or during alliance wars
  • Don't launch attacks FROM the safehouse (breaks neutrality)
  • Return to normal position once threat has passed
  • Limited space - priority for members most at risk

Important: The safehouse location is marked in the alliance bookmarks.

Critical Requirements

No attacks on other alliances without talking to the leaders first.

Inactive without letting us know will result in a kick from the alliance.

Everyone must check the alliance threads regarding attacking policies.

These rules are essential to follow or else you will be kicked out.

For questions or clarifications, contact alliance leaders in-game.

Level 30 - 45: Event Focus & Resource Collection

1

Main Kingdom Focus

Focus only on main kingdom for now. Must buy timeskips before the blacksmith resets.

2

Use Timeskips Wisely

Use timeskips for upgrading buildings and completing quests efficiently.

3

Priority Quests

Try to finish the Princess Quest (most rewarding) and the new building quests.

4

Event Upgrades

Upgrade as much as possible during events. You will face resource shortages for some time.

5

Outpost Strategy

Focus outposts on food production only. Upgrade food buildings to store troops.

6

Event Difficulty

Use maximum difficulty available. Don't do defending tasks (townships/taunting khan).

Level 45 - 62/63: Expand to Other Kingdoms

1

Storehouse First

Upgrade storehouse to maximum level to send maximum resources through main kingdom.

2

Tower Attacks

Send troops to other kingdoms to attack towers (barbarian, cult, etc.) for XP quests.

3

Aquamarine Kingdom

Focus on Aquamarine Kingdom - it gives free XP.

4

Village Level 2

Build village to level 2 on each kingdom.

5

Resource Buildings

Build kingdom-specific resource buildings for donation quest XP.

6

Expected Progress

Following these steps will easily get you to level 60-61.

7

Event Difficulty

Use maximum difficulty available. Don't do defending tasks (townships/taunting khan).
