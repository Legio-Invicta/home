Dear new members of Legio Invicta, this guide presents all major events in Goodgame Empire. Whether you're a beginner or experienced player, you'll find all the information you need to excel in each event.

Beyond the Horizon

Level 30+

Requirements

  • Must be in alliance before event starts
  • Enter Horizon within first 48 hours (2-day joining window)
  • First player to register becomes leader of first Horizon Alliance (max 22 players)
  • Up to 3 Horizon alliances per main alliance (approx. 60 players total)

Objective

Occupy states and defeat opponents' capitals/castles to gain Influence. Daily influence transfers after midnight to your alliance capital, which must be defended during the final days.

Influence counts for individual and alliance leaderboards. Final alliance rank equals influence in capital at event end.

Key Features

Cult Shrine

Works like robber barons. Levels up after defeat and yields more resources next time.

Outposts

Build and occupy outposts, but they vanish post-event (temporary server).

Observation Towers

Provide attack bonuses against capitals and city-states. Maximum 5 per alliance.

City-State

100k influence on capture with 8-hour timer. Max 5 per alliance. Upgrade shrine with rubies to reduce time.

Capital

One per alliance, tied to leader. Daily influence is vaulted here. Defend against plunders.

Outer Realms

Level 70+

Important Information

  • Special event on temporary server
  • Individual entry (no alliance requirement upfront, but alliances can be formed in-event)
  • Unused rubies transfer back to main account post-event
  • No troops, tools, or resources carry over

Objective

Compete in rotating scoring modes by attacking castles, collecting points, constructing/upgrading buildings, or swapping ranks. Daily rankings at midnight determine progress and eligibility. Final ranks award rewards like relic equipment, troops, tokens, and decorations.

Scoring Modes

Heritage Hunter

Collect Heritage Points by plundering castles. Each castle has fixed points - gain 10% portion, boostable with rubies.

No resource theft. Tools and soldiers return instantly.

Snappy Swap

Attack higher-ranked players to swap positions. Victory equals swap plus resource loot.

Distances fixed at 25. Daily resets.

Masters of Might

Earn Might Points via building and upgrading (including decorations).

Fires exclude buildings from count. Resources are lootable.

Additional Features

  • Immediate troop and tool return post-battle
  • Alliances available for support
  • Quests for progression
  • No Emperor's Palace in some modes
  • Robber Barons and Castles: Attack for resources and points, build defenses

Nomad Invasion

Level 30+

Event Type

NPC Event (player vs environment). No alliance requirement - individual participation. The Herald of the Invasion calls upon castle lords to defeat nomad invaders who have set up camps in the Great Empire.

Objective

During the event, 4 Nomad Camps spawn near your main castle. Attack them without cooldown up to 10 times each for points and loot. Camps have unique defenders (e.g., slingers, saber cleavers). Higher levels yield better rewards.

Key Features

Nomad Camps

4 personal camps. Infinite attacks but limited to 10 hits per camp, then cooldown starts.

Khan Camp

Khan's Revenge (Alliance Add-on): Alliance leader gets a Khan Camp nearby. Attack to earn Rage Points and trigger revenge attacks on your castle for Khan Medals (trade for items).

Khan Tablets

Event currency obtained from camps and Khan. Trade at Herald's Shop for troops, tools, resources, rubies, relic gear, and decorations.

Unique Units

Nomad-specific defenders and attackers add strategic variety to battles.

Rankings

Points determine individual and alliance ranks for rewards. Top ranks receive rubies and premium items.

Pro Tip - Legio Invicta Strategy

Buy Horsetails with your Khan Tablets and stack them for events like War of Realms.

Use them if you're level 70 during glory events. Use the easiest difficulty for War of Realms and Bloodcrow event - this is the fastest way to level up after reaching level 70.

Samurai Invasion

Level 10+

Requirements

Alliance event where players attack Samurai Camps that spawn around the map to earn event points. You must be in an alliance before the event starts to participate fully and qualify for alliance rewards (individual play possible but limited rewards).

Objective

Defeat Samurai Camps (similar to Nomad Camps but themed with Samurai units) to collect points. Camps level up after attacks, becoming harder but yielding more points and rewards. Higher difficulty levels offer better loot.

Alliance coordination for mass attacks maximizes points and leaderboard positions.

Key Features

Samurai Camps

Spawn near player castles. Attack repeatedly to level them up for better rewards.

Difficulty System

Choose camp and challenge level for balanced farming and optimal point gains.

Rewards

Alliance rewards unlock after at least one camp is defeated. Top alliances get premium troops and relic items.

Rankings

Points count for individual and alliance leaderboards. Top ranks receive rubies, Samurai units, tools, resources, and decorations.

Strategy Tips

  • Coordinate with alliance members for synchronized attacks
  • Focus on higher difficulty camps for maximum point efficiency
  • Balance individual points with alliance contribution
  • Utilize event-specific Samurai troops for strategic advantages

Berimond

Level 40+

Event Type

Faction-based event in a separate kingdom (Berimond). Players are automatically assigned to one of two factions (House of Gerbrandt - loyal to Leopold III, or House of Ursidae - loyal to Grimbert) based on alliance affiliation. Entire alliances are grouped together.

Duration: 3-7 days

Objective

Players build camps and fight in lanes of watchtowers to destroy the enemy capital. Factions compete to conquer towers, resource villages, and the opposing capital, leading to a "Last Man Standing" phase for the losing side.

Earn Gallantry Points from attacks. Rankings determine titles and rewards.

Key Features

Camps

Player bases (like simplified castles). Build tents for soldier limits (reduces morale). Boost morale with decorations. Public Order is replaced by Morale.

Watchtowers

3 lanes (Top/Middle/Bottom) with multiple towers to destroy via victory attacks. Example: Tower 1 requires 27 successful attacks.

Kings and Capitals

Fight for Leopold III or Grimbert. Destroy enemy capital to win the event.

Gallantry Points

Earned from tower and camp attacks. Unlock titles, Imperial troops (stronger than Kingsguard), and bonuses. Boost with tools and rubies.

Resource Villages

Capturable after nearby tower is destroyed. Provide resources and soldiers to strengthen your faction.

Mechanics

Transfer soldiers and tools from main server. No honor loss. Park troops at capitals for safety. Attack-back available for defense.

Alliance Rules & Policies

Essential guidelines for all Legio Invicta members. Failure to follow these rules may result in removal from the alliance.

PVP (Player vs Player) Policies

1

Non-Alliance Players

You don't need any sort of permission to attack a non-alliance player. They are free to attack.

2

Other Alliances' Territory

No attacking or capturing any sort of outposts, resource villages, or monuments of other alliances.

3

Alliance Member Attacks

Before attacking a member of another alliance, make sure to consult the leader or deputy.

4

Attack Frequency

No attacks on a single player twice a week unless that person is inactive.

5

Level Restrictions

Don't attack anyone 10 levels or more below you.

Troop Safety - Safehouse Protocol

When Going Offline

Make sure to send your attacking troops to the bookmark place named "Safehouse". This way your attacking troops won't die. Keep your defending troops in the castle. This ensures your attacking troops will stay safe.

Timing Guidelines

Those who are staying at long distances from us should send troops for less amount of time. For example, if going offline for 6 hours, send your troops only for 5 hours, etc.

Important: The safehouse location is marked in the alliance bookmarks.

Critical Requirements

No attacks on other alliances without talking to the leaders first.

Inactive without letting us know will result in a kick from the alliance.

Everyone must check the alliance threads regarding attacking policies.

These rules are essential to follow or else you will be kicked out.

For questions or clarifications, contact alliance leaders in-game.
